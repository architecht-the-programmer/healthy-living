Good Food..

Healthy Eating!!

Good health is impossible without good nutrition and good nutrition is only possible through a balanced diet.A healthful diet must be one that is complete,sufficient and balanced.This means one must include at least one food from each of the three main food groups in each meal and vary what they eat.So what are the 3 main food groups??

The 3 main food groups

  • The first group is the carbohydrate group which supplies quick energy.This group includes cereals such as corn,wheat,rice,oats,rye,barley and millet.Tubers such as yams and potatoes also fall under this group.
  • The second group are foods that are a source of protein,iron,zinc and several vitamins.These foods include legumes such as beans,soyabeans,lentils,chickpeas and some foods of animal origin such as meat,fish,eggs and milk with its derivatives.
  • The third group provides essential vitamins and minerals as well as fiber and energy and they represent the only natural source of vitamin C.Fruits and vegetables fall under this group.

    • Eat fresh and whole foods!!

    Concentrate on eating "real" food - whole, fresh foods. Whole grain foods are good and richer in nutrients and fiber than refined foods,so when buying bread, cereals, pasta or rice go for the whole-grains.

    TIP!!

    Parents protect your children's health by helping them acquire a taste for healthy food.For example,instead of giving them candy give them nuts or fresh fruits and vegetables.

    A MUST try breakfast recipe!!
    How to make avocado toast with eggs

    -Toast your bread(preferably brown bread) and allow it to cool.
    -In a bowl,mash some avocado.You can also add salt and pepper to the mash for better flavour.Set this aside.

    -Heat a large non-sticky frying pan to a setting just above medium to get it ready.
    -In a large mixing bowl,whisk eggs for about 2 minutes.

    -Melt butter in the heated pan.As the last of the butter liquifies,add the egg mixture aiming for the center of the pan.Do not stir immediately.Wait till the first hint of setting begins,then gently fold the eggs from one side of the pan to the other.You’ll get soft fluffy eggs without adding any milk.
    -Thereafter,you can spread your mashed avocado on your toasted bread and add the eggs as well.

    **Enjoy your tasty,healthy breakfast**

    Tips for making the best avocado toast with egg

    • 1. Pick ripe avocados. It all comes down to the avocados. Try to find an avocado that’s ripe, but not over-ripe. You’ll know it’s ripe if you gently squeeze the avocado with the palm of your hands and it yields a little. Don’t use your fingertips because that might bruise it.

    • 2. Use thick-sliced bread and toast it. It’s best to buy good bread that’s strong enough to hold the avocado and eggs and other toppings you might like to add. Use something that offers a sturdy base like a thick multi-grain bread.

    • 3. Mash the avocado with add-ins for more flavor. You can add lemon or lime juice with salt and pepper. You can also add fresh herbs like cilantro, parsley or basil and spices like crushed red pepper, cumin or paprika. It’s almost like a very simple guacamole ready to be spread on toast.

    Do not eat too much!!

    How much you eat can be as important as what you eat,so while enjoying your meal also learn to listen to your stomach.This means you will stop eating when you're full and you will avoid bad eating habits such as going back for seconds.Habitually eating or drinking to excess is not only bad for your health but can also be a problem on your finances.Therefore,controlling your eating habits is beneficial in many ways.

    Healthy fluids..
    Fresh juice

    Fresh juices are packed with soluble fiber, a type of fiber that supports good bacteria growth and digestive health. It has been shown to regulate blood sugar levels, lower cholesterol, and slow the transit of food through the digestive tract.

    Antioxidants occur naturally in plant-based foods and help prevent or stop cell damage caused by oxidants. Freshly squeezed juices are a great way to consume these.

    Herbal tea

    Herbal tea is 'THE' drink if you wish to detoxify your body. Given the amount of toxines contained in the food we eat nowadays, we need to detoxify our body on a regular basis to enjoy a healthy life.

    No matter how slim a person is, they would always want to lose more weight. Herbal tea is rich in functional ingredients like physillium husk, fennel and lemongrass which help you to burn fats and also helps in boosting metabolism.

    Lemon water

    Vitamin C found in lemons may help reduce skin wrinkling, dry skin from aging, and damage from the sun. How water improves skin is controversial, but one thing is certain. If your skin loses moisture, it becomes dry and prone to wrinkles.

    Research has shown that polyphenol antioxidants found in lemons significantly reduces weight gain. Although some researchers have contradicted, lemon water has been recommended by many.
